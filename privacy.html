Privacy Policy

Edge Runner is a simple single-player game. It does not collect, store, or share personal data.


Data we collect

None. No account info, no analytics, no tracking cookies.


Local storage

By default, the game does not need to save anything. If you add optional settings later (like a volume toggle), it may use browser storage on your device. That would stay on your device.


Third-party services

This site loads fonts from Google Fonts. That request is handled by your browser.
