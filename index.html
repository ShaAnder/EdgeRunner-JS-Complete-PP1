How it works

  1. Hit Start Run (or press a control key) to begin.
  2. Move with A/D (or ←/→).
  3. Press Space to jump. Hold ↑ to charge a higher jump.
  4. Avoid spikes. Survive longer to boost speed and score.

Controls: A/D or ←/→ to move, Space to jump, hold ↑ to charge. Touch: hold left/right to move, tap/hold center to jump.
